Broadway & Thomas

Broadway & Thomas: The project at Broadway and Thomas has 7,930 SF of retail space, 39,370 SF of office space, and is home to several county of Los Angeles offices, a Beyond the Pahramacy Rx, and an upcoming BurgerIM. This project also includes a dedicated parking structure of 3 floors for retail and office use. Neptune Waterproofing was selected to make sure the entirety of the county building is waterproofed. Sepulveda & Western Starbucks

1644 W Sepulveda Blvd: Neptune Waterproofing has been a reliable client for Starbuck's waterproofing needs.
